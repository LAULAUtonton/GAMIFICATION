Present Simple

We use it for routines, facts and habits.

Example: She plays tennis every weekend.

Quick Quiz

He usually gets up at 7.
He usually get up at 7.

Present Continuous

We use it for actions happening now.

Example: They are watching a movie.

Quick Quiz

She is play football.
She is playing football.

Pronouns

Subject: I, you, he, she, it, we, they

Object: me, you, him, her, it, us, them

Quick Quiz

I saw her yesterday.
I saw she yesterday.

Verb To Be

am / is / are

Example: They are happy.

Quick Quiz

She is a teacher.
She are a teacher.

There is / There are

Use “there is” for singular, “there are” for plural.

Quick Quiz

There is a cat on the table.
There are a cat on the table.

There was / There were

Past form of “there is / there are”.

Quick Quiz

There were a big storm yesterday.
There was a big storm yesterday.
